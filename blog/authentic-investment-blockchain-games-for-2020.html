Authentic Investment Blockchain Games for 2020
Created at: Wed Jun 03 2020
|
Updated at: Sun Nov 01 2020

Blockchain games are any games that include blockchain technology in its backend or mechanics in general. Blockchain technology is powering the future of gaming, allowing players to trade, buy, sell and discover digital items.

The most often use for blockchain technology in games so far has been been to store your items on the blockchain, tying them to your cryptocurrency wallet and making them your own. In other words, your items are decentralized. Instead of living in a centrally controlled server, a blockchain game items (digital assets) are distributed among players.

Acronyms and their meanings: DApp = Decentralized Application; MMO = Massively Multiplayer Online; RPG = Role-Playing Game; PvP = Player(s) versus Player(s); PvE = Player versus Environment/Monster
